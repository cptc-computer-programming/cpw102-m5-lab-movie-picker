# Movie Night Recommender

## Goal

Write a program that recommends a movie based on user input.

*If you and your partner don't watch movies, then pick another media, like TV shows, video games, or books*

---

## What Your Program Must Do

- Ask the user at least **2 questions**
- Use:
  - at least **1 qualitative input** (text like mood, genre, etc.)
  - at least **1 numeric input** (number like time, age, etc.)
- Recommend **at least 5 different movies**
- Store the recommendation in a variable
- Print the recommendation **only at the end**
- Include a **default movie** (in case nothing matches)
- Have a clear greeting and clean output

---

## Decision Requirements

Your program must include:

- at least one `if / elif / else` chain (3+ branches)
- at least one **nested decision**
- at least one **logical operator** (`and`, `or`, `not`)
- at least one **boolean variable**

Example ideas:
- combine inputs: `if mood == "happy" and time < 90`
- boolean variable: `isGroup = (numPeople > 1)`

---

## Input Ideas

You can choose your own inputs.

### Qualitative Inputs
- mood
- genre
- who you're watching with
- energy level
- wants something funny (yes/no)

### Numeric Inputs
- time available (minutes)
- number of people
- age
- hour of day
- tiredness (1–5)

---

## Phase 1: Design

Before coding:

- Create `DESIGN.md`
- Use the template provided
- Complete and commit it **before writing code**

You must include:
- your inputs
- your movies (5+)
- your default movie
- your boolean variable
- your plan for decisions
- your decision outline
- at least 5 test cases

---

## Phase 2: Code

Build your program.

- ask the user questions
- use decisions to pick a movie
- store the result in a variable
- print the result at the end
